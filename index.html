Your Dream Vacation?

Vacation planning sucks. There's so much time spent on things that get out of the way of the fun. Why not let us do the boring stuff while you focus your time sorely on the fun stuff. Any kind of vacation you want to go to, be it a trip to the beach, a tour of an exotic place abroad, camping trip, a cruise, a group tour or a visit to a National park. We've got you covered.

Create Vacation and Add Users

You can create a vacation plan tht suits your taste and add users, be it friends, family members colleagues etc to join you on that vacation plan.

Suggest a vacation trip

You can suggest the kind of vacation trip you want to embark on, different places you want to travel to and various sites you want to visit.

Suggest Dates and Activities

You can suggest things to do while on the vacation, the different dates you want to go on these activities. Share these suggestions and dates with others who are added to your vacation plan and they also can make suggestions on dates and activities as well.

Message and comment

This app lets you comment on vacations and trip activities created by others and yourself. You can also like vacations plans made by others. There's also a instant messaging feature which enables you chat and message others on the app.
